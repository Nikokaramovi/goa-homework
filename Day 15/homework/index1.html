about goa Hierarchy

student

A student who enters the Leadership Academy
to master programming languages.

Minileader

The mini leader is responsible for helping the children in
his squad leader's squad (to make things easier for his leader)

Squad leader

The squad leader is responsible for recruiting children into
his squad and monitoring them daily (as well as providing information to parents).

Minileader Controller

Minileader controllers monitor minileaders, check how well they
help children, and check whether they play games.

GitHub Controller

GitHub Controller inspects all children in GitHub Academy
twice a month and issues fines to their leaders.

Squad Leader Controller

The squad leader controller supervises all squad leaders, checking on
a daily basis how well they are performing their work.

Mentor

The mentor conducts lessons at the highest level and provides
children with information on programming languages.
